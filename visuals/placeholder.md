# Visuals

This folder is reserved for visual products supporting the analyses in `/analysis`. As with the rest of this project, planned visuals are based on already-published AIS-platform outputs and reporting — not on original real-time tracking infrastructure.

## Planned Content

- **Bosphorus and Dardanelles traffic map** — illustrating the geographic chokepoint, sourced from VTS/public geographic data, not live AIS feeds
- **Shadow-fleet vessel movement chart** — summarizing documented flag-state and AIS-anomaly patterns from Case Study 2 (Analysis 3), with per-data-point sourcing to CREA/Lloyd's List rather than original tracking
- **Black Sea Grain Initiative corridor map** — showing the designated corridor and documented traffic decline after July 2023, sourced to UN JCC documentation and Reuters reporting

## Status

These visuals are planned, not yet produced. Each will cite the specific case study and source it is drawn from, consistent with the sourcing discipline used throughout `/analysis`.
